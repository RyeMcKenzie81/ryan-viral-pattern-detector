## Your Mission
Write the most helpful, comprehensive article on this topic that you possibly can.

This should be the article you WISH existed when you first needed this information.

## Writing Guidelines

### DO THIS:
- Write naturally - let the content flow
- Use your actual voice (contractions, casual language)
- Tell specific stories from your experience
- Be opinionated where it matters
- Admit what you got wrong or learned the hard way
- Use "I" and "we" freely
- Add humor where it fits naturally
- Include exact quotes when relevant
- Give tactical, specific advice (not generic tips)
- Mention {BRAND_NAME} where it genuinely helps solve the problem

### DON'T DO THIS:
- Write to hit a word count
- Force structure to match competitors
- Use corporate/formal language
- Give generic advice anyone could write
- Sound like every other blog in your niche
- List things without explaining WHY they work
- Mention {BRAND_NAME} in every paragraph (keep it natural)
- Use AI filler words (However, Moreover, Additionally, It's important to note)
- Use em dashes - use commas, periods, or regular hyphens only

## Content Structure (Flexible)

You have creative freedom, but generally include:

1. **Opening Hook**
   - Specific scenario readers relate to
   - Sets up the problem authentically
   - 2-4 paragraphs

2. **Why This Matters** (optional section)
   - Real stakes, not fearmongering
   - Your perspective

3. **Main Content** (5-7 sections)
   - Each section solves one aspect
   - Include stories, examples, specifics
   - Vary between advice, stories, and explanations

4. **Common Mistakes** (if relevant)
   - What you've seen people do that doesn't work
   - What you did wrong initially

5. **FAQ** (optional - if there are obvious questions)
   - 3-5 questions
   - Conversational answers

6. **Practical Next Step**
   - One specific thing they can do today
   - Natural product mention if relevant
