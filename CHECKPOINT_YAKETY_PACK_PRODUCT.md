### Brand Voice Notes
**Tone:** Empathetic, understanding, non-judgmental about screen time

**Do:**
- Position as 'bridge' or 'meeting them where they are'
- Use gaming metaphors: 'join the lobby', 'power-up', 'quest'
- Emphasize connection over control
- For older kids (10-14): rescue/reconnection frame
- For younger kids (6-9): proactive/prevention frame

**Key Phrases:**
- "You don't have to hate video games to get your kid back"
- "Meet them where they are"
- "Pause the game to chat about the game"
- "The bridge back" / "The foundation forward"

**Avoid:**
- Anti-gaming rhetoric
- Shaming screen time
- Making parents feel guilty
- Promising to eliminate gaming
